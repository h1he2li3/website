CO2 Onboard

At CO2 Onboard, our aim is to decarbonize the energy system while ensuring minimal change to the current infrastructure but still closing the emissions loop between fuel production and fuel usage.

We focus on developing powertrains for large transportation applications and stationary power generation systems, which enable net-zero and even net-negative emissions, depending on the fuel source.

Our system is designed to manage emission cycle right at the point of use. It produces power while also capturing, and storing valuable CO2 emissions for later use. These high value emissions can be used as raw materials in fuel production, chemical, agriculture, food, etc. industries.

Additionally, the captured CO2 can also be passed on to companies that deal with long-term carbon storage.
